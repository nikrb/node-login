Getting Started

First you should set up your team player names. To do this select Team from the home screen. Click on a player and then either:

  1. Enter a name and email, or
  2. click select contact if the player is in your contacts list. Select the contact followed by the appropriate email. Note you can only select a contact that has atleast one email.

To start creating games and entering shots, see the game tutorial accessed from the tutorial menu above.

Sync/Save Operations

You should sign up for an account if you wish to use sync/save features, see below.

Check out the tutorials, accessed from the menu above. There are two tutorials covering the sync facility from the perspective of a coach and player.

Account Creation

Click Login on the home screen and then click Create Account on the login screen. Enter your details and click submit. You will then be taken back to the login screen and you should login with the user/pass you entered for your account.
