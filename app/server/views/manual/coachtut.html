Coach Sync Tutorial

This tutorial guides the user through first app start-up and registering to viewing routines a player has completed on request

Terms

  • Workout - a collection of drills
  • Drill - a description of an exercise to increase basketball skill
  • Practice - imlpementation of a workout. i.e. a player actually performs the workout drills as an exercise and notes outcomes for each
  • Outcome - player results of performing a drill. Reps and shot made for the drill.
  • Routine - a workout that is requested by a coach for a player to carry out. In most cases, a synonym for workout.

Home Screen

Step One: Register

First start up the app. On the home screen click the login button at the top right. When the login screen appears, click the Create Account button. Fill in your details and hit submit. You should now be registered

Step Two: Login

Go back to the login screen, enter your details and click sign in. Click remember-me to login automatically next time.

Step Three: Name Player(s)

In order to send a routine to a player, the player needs an email address. We will just edit one player for the purposes of this tutorial. Note the routine isn't sent via email, but in the system; email uniquely identifies someone.

From the home screen, click the Team Button and you should see a list of players. Click on the first player and you should see the player details. Either select a player from your contacts list, or enter their name and email manually.

Click the save button and return to the home screen.

Step Four: Create a Workout

From the home screen, click the Workouts button. The workout list should be empty. Click the plus (+) sign at the top right to create a new workout.

Enter a name and a description for the workout, then click the plus (+) sign next to the Drill list title, to select the drills for this workout.

Select the top two drills for the purposes of this tutorial. Then click Done to return to the workout detail view and click Save.

Step Five: Assign Workout to Player(s)

You should still be in the workouts screen, if not, click the workouts button in the home screen.

Click the envelope icon on the workout line to assign a requested routine to a player(s).

Select the player we entered the email for and click Save. You can select multiple players here, but for this tutorial we only have one player with an email.

The workout list should now contain an entry in the Requested section. Note the user the routine is destined for.

Step Six: Perform Sync

To actually send the workout to players we need to perform a sync. There are two types of sync, coach and player. Player sync lets players retrieve workouts requested by their coach and return them when they have completed them. Coach sync lets coach send workouts to players and retrieve them to view the outcome when the player has completed them. We will be using coach sync for this tutorial.

Return to the home page and ensure we are logged in. The home screen should display your name and logout in the top right if you are logged in, if login is displayed, click it and login. From the home screen, click the sync button. You will now see the sync screen with a whirring activity indicator, when it stops whirring you will see the list of requested routines.

Click the Coach Sync button and the activity indicator will start to whir again. When it stops whirring you should see the sent routine listed, see image below.

Note the sync output:

  • No New Drills To Process - We didn't create any new drills
  • Routine:workout 1 [requested] to [bea@gmail.com] and ok - The routine was sent successfully
  • Workout:workout 1 - We created a new workout, which was successfully sent
  • No Completed Routines Found - There were no player completed routines to fetch

Step Seven: Wait

Now we wait for the targetted player to complete the routine and return it to us. This will be covered in another tutorial

Step Eight: Retrieve Completed Routines

From time to time you will want to check for any routine requests completed by players. Ensure you are logged in at the home screen, then click the sync button. Wait for the activity indicator to stop whirring and then click the coach sync button. The activity indicator will start to whir again and when it stops, any returned routines will be listed, see image below.

Step Nine: Check Outcomes

Finally, we want to see how well the player performed the requested routines. Navigate to the home page and click the Practice Reports button. This will take you to the practice list, as shown below.

Notice the practice list has three sections.

  • Orphan - this shows practices who's workouts have been deleted.
  • Workout Name - Each workout is listed as a section, with each practice performed and the date. This shows practices completed by me, meaning me as a player having practiced a workout locally using my device - in other words, private player practices that were not requested by coach
  • Routines - This section lists the workout practices completed and returned by players to their coach.
For the purposes of the tutorial, we have one listed in the requested section. In order to see the outcome, click on the routine and you will be presented with a view showing the outcome for each drill in the workout.

That concludes this tutorial, thanx for participating.
